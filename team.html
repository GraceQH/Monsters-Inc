The city of Monstropolis in the monster world is powered by energy from the screams of human children.

At the Monsters, Inc. factory, skilled monsters employed as scarers venture into the human world to scare children and harvest their screams, through doors that activate portals to the children's bedroom closets. It is considered dangerous work, as human children are believed to be toxic.

James P. Sullivan (Scarer)

Sulley is an imposing monster,
measuring 7’6’’ and weighing
768 pounds according to both
of his Scare Cards.

Sulley is a top scarer under
his boss Henry J. Waternoose, III,
who is the CEO of Monsters, Inc.

Mike Wazoski (Assistant)

Mike Wazowski is a proud and
confident monster, partly
defined by his friendship with
James P. Sullivan, whom he
works with as his Scaring
Assistant.

He is a light green, spherical
monster with one large green
eye, two small horns on his head.

Randall Boggs (Scarer)

Randall has 3 fronds, green eyes,
4 arms and legs and has the
ability to completely blend in
with his surroundings, making
him invisible (as well as being
able to cling to walls).

He can also camouflage similar to
the way chameleons are
misconceived to.

Jeff Fungus (Assistant)

He is a small, red monster
with three eyes and two legs
similar to those of a chicken.
He wears three-lensed glasses.

Fungus doesn't stand up for
himself very often, mainly because
he is scared of Randall due to
his explosive temper.

George Sanderson (Scarer)

George is the 8th best scarer on
Scarefloor F at Monsters, Inc.
He is a furry monster with a
horn on top of his head.

He is a monster that runs afoul of
the CDA's "Code 2319" (contact with
a human child) several times, forcing
the CDA to shave and scrub him to
remove any trace of human contact.

Charlie Proctor (Assistant)

Assistant of George Sanderson,
Charlie has blue skin and two octopus
like arms, and also four tentacles
that act as feet. He has snail
eyes that pass through two holes
in his workman's hat.

Celia Mae (Receptionist)

Celia is the receptionist at
Monsters, Inc., which means she
takes calls by monsters and may
click buttons for monster voicemail.

Celia is a slender light magenta
monster with short purple hair
that resemble snakes.

Roz Foster (Administrator)

She is the key master and admin
for Scare Floor F, holding all the
keys to children's closet doors at
Monsters, Inc.

She is responsible for receiving
all of the paperwork done by
Mike Wazowski, and she often
works in an office near Scare
Floor F.
